How this number is actually calculated

Rates come from the Frankfurter v1 API, a free, keyless service that redistributes the European Central Bank's daily reference rates. The ECB only publishes rates against the euro — so any pair not involving EUR is a cross-rate, computed by dividing one euro rate by another, shown above under "how this number is calculated."

No rate is published on weekends or ECB holidays; the tool always shows the actual date used, not the date you asked for. Educational tool, not financial advice — see the Citation Ledger in the repo for full sourcing.
